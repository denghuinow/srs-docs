# Ultra Short Summary

The Clarus system is a nationwide initiative to collect, quality-check, and disseminate surface transportation environmental data (weather, pavement, and water conditions) to enhance safety, mobility, and forecasting.

**MVP Points**
- The system shall collect, quality check, and disseminate environmental data from multiple sources.
- The system shall implement quality checking processes as soon as data become available.
- The system shall provide environmental data quality flags.
- The system shall be able to restrict environmental data publication based on source.

**Key Constraints**
- The system shall use industry standards to minimize implementation impact to users and providers.
- The system shall be able to be hosted at one or more physical locations.
- The system shall use Coordinated Universal Time (UTC) for all timestamps.

**Major Risks/Undecided Issues**
- The usefulness of the system is dependent on participation by multiple environmental data providers and consumers.
