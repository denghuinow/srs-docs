# Ultra Short Summary
A regional center-to-center communications network for sharing traffic data and device control across Dallas/Ft. Worth traffic management centers.

- **MVP points**
  - Provide a common repository for traffic information in the DFW Metroplex.
  - Display traffic conditions via a World Wide Web-based graphical map.
  - Enable agencies without formal Traffic Management Centers to participate through a Microsoft Windows application.
  - Support command, control, and status of ITS field devices using National ITS standards.

- **Key constraints**
  - The system must utilize National ITS standards for implementation.
  - The C2C Server shall execute in a Microsoft Windows NT environment.
  - The system shall be extensible to local, regional, and statewide partners.
