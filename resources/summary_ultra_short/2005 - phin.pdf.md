# Ultra Short Summary
A system to support investigation, monitoring, management, analysis, and reporting of public health outbreaks and bioterrorism events.

- **MVP points**
  - Collect and link data for cases, exposures, contacts, specimens, and laboratory results.
  - Support configurable electronic questionnaires and dynamic data fields for different health events.
  - Enable contact exposure tracing and relationship mapping between entities.
  - Generate analysis, visualization, and reports for event monitoring and response.

- **Key constraints**
  - Must use established vocabulary and data exchange standards for interoperability.
  - Must ensure data security, privacy, and protection from unauthorized access.
  - Must support multiple deployment modes including disconnected operation with synchronization.
