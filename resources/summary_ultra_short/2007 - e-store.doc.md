# Ultra Short Summary

An e‑store for Marvel Electronics and Home Entertainment enabling online sales, distribution, and marketing of electronics.

**MVP points**
- Allow online purchase of products with a shopping cart and multiple payment methods.
- Provide product search, detailed categorization, and comprehensive product details.
- Maintain customer profiles with order history and personalized features.
- Support order confirmation, email notifications, and invoice generation.

**Key constraints**
- The system must be web‑based and accessible via standard browsers.
- Development must follow standard web page tools conforming to IBM CUA or Microsoft GUI standards.
- The system must use HTTP protocol and port 80 for communication.
