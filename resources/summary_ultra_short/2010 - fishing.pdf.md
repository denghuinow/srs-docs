# Ultra Short Summary

The UK Fishing Vessel’s Electronic Logbook system enables EC fishing vessels over 15 meters to electronically record and report fishing activities to comply with Council Regulation (EC) No. 1966/2006.

- **MVP points**
  - Capture all required fishing activity data and output it as a validated XML file.
  - Transmit data via encrypted email to the UK fisheries administrations’ ERS system.
  - Support mandatory report types including Departure, Fishing Activity, and Landing Declaration.
  - Provide printing capabilities for logbook and landing declaration data.

- **Key constraints**
  - Must use English (UK) localization and UTC for all dates and times.
  - Must retain all logbook reports and corrections until the end of each trip.
  - Must only be supplied for onboard use and not for onshore agents.
