# Ultra Short Summary
A video search engine that searches multiple websites for streaming videos and torrents to help users quickly find desired content.

**MVP points**
- Search for torrents and streaming videos from multiple websites using a single query.
- Display results in separate tabs with sorting by name, size, date, and other categories.
- Allow users to filter results by content type, age restrictions, and specific websites.
- Provide options to save favorite videos for later access.

**Key constraints**
- Must be portable across Windows, Mac, and Linux operating systems.
- Requires an active internet connection to function.
- Database of searchable websites must be maintained and updated by developers.

**Major risks/undecided issues**
- Legal review required before public release due to potential liability concerns.
