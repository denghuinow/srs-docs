# Ultra Short Summary

A GIS-based system for tracking and analyzing water use permit data to support regulatory and resource management activities in Southwest Florida.

**MVP Points**
- The system shall track geographic and temporal trends in permitted and actual water use.
- The system shall provide tools to aggregate water use data over defined geographic areas.
- The system shall support analysis of water use impacts on Minimum Flows and Levels (MFL).
- The system shall enable viewing and reporting of water use permit information.

**Key Constraints**
- The system must operate within the District's existing hardware and software architecture.
- The system is dependent on data from existing Regulatory, Water Management, and GIS databases.
- The system must replicate DB2 data daily to an Oracle database.
