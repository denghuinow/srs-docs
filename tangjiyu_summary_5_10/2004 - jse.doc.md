# 2004 - jse.md

## System Overview
The JSE (Johannesburg Stock Exchange) ISIN (International Securities Identification Number) Issuance system is designed to automate the issuance, amendment, and deletion of ISIN codes for money market instruments (MMIs) in South Africa, enabling electronic trading and settlement on the same day.^[1]^

## Key Requirements

### Business Requirements
- **Objective**: Enable electronic issuance, trading, and settlement of MMIs.^[2]^
- **Scope**: Provide an electronic method for MMF participants to create, amend, and delete ISIN codes via STRATE.^[3]^
- **MMF Requirements**: 
  - Reduce risks, improve liquidity, tradability, and reduce costs in the Money Market.^[4]^
  - Align with global best practices and improve market integrity.^[5]^
  - Immediate ISIN issuance for trading and settlement.^[6]^
  - Estimated 200-500 ISINs per day.^[7]^
  - Use of ZAM prefix for MMIs.^[8]^

### Functional Requirements
- **ISIN Issuance**: Automatically issue ISINs in response to SWIFT requests.^[9]^
- **Message Handling**: 
  - Handle SWIFT message types for ISIN creation, cancellation, deregistration, and their respective rejections and confirmations.
  - Use MT 598 User Defined SWIFT messages for communication.^[10]^
- **System Integration**: 
  - Interact with STRATE for message routing.^[11]^
  - Apply for a new BIC code extension (ISN) for correct routing.^[12]^

### Data Requirements
- **Data Elements**: 
  - Issuer, MMI Type, Short Description, MMI Generic Category, Issue Date, Maturity Date, Coupon Rate, Coupon Interval.^[13]^
- **Message Content**: 
  - Define fields and contents for MT 598 messages.^[14]^
  - Include transaction reference, preparation date and time, type of instruction, financial instrument attributes, and identification of securities.^[15]^

### Service Level Agreement
- **Performance**: 
  - Develop a program for automatic ISIN issuance.^[16]^
  - Ensure timely processing and response to SWIFT messages.^[17]^
- **Data Management**: 
  - Maintain accurate and up-to-date data for each MMI.
  - Adhere to ANNA and ISO guidelines for ISIN issuance and deletion.^[18]^
