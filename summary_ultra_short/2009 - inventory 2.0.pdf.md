# Ultra Short Summary
An inventory management system for Construction Junction to categorize, track, and manage donated items from donation through sale.

- **MVP points**
  - Allow staff to view and navigate inventory by department and category.
  - Enable receiving associates to process donations and generate receipts.
  - Support adding and managing unique and stock items in the inventory.
  - Integrate with QuickBooks POS for sales and inventory updates.

- **Key constraints**
  - Must integrate with QuickBooks POS and Salesforce CRM.
  - The system must be usable via touch screen workstations.
  - All inventory updates must be promptly reflected in QuickBooks POS.
