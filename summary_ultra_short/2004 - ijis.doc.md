# Ultra Short Summary
A system to manage indigent defense services for Tarrant County's Integrated Justice Information System, enabling efficient appointment and oversight of court-appointed attorneys.

- **MVP points**
  - Submit and process requests for appointed counsel electronically.
  - Notify defendants and attorneys of appointments and case updates.
  - Manage attorney lists and appointment rotations.
  - Generate compliance reports for the Texas Fair Defense Act.

- **Key constraints**
  - Must comply with Texas Code of Criminal Procedure and Texas Government Code.
  - Requires integration with external Court Case Management and accounting systems.
  - Must adhere to Texas Department of Information Resources guidelines for electronic records.
