# Ultra Short Summary

The Tactical Control System (TCS) is a joint-service command and control system providing interoperable control, data processing, and dissemination for tactical unmanned aerial vehicles and their payloads.

**MVP Points**
- Control Predator and Outrider UAVs across five levels of interaction from data receipt to full flight control.
- Perform mission planning, real-time mission control/monitoring, and payload product management.
- Interface with multiple C4I systems for tasking and data dissemination.
- Process and display EO/IR/SAR payload imagery in compliance with NITF 2.0 and CIGSS standards.

**Key Constraints**
- Must use DII/COE-compliant open architecture and non-proprietary software.
- Hardware must be scalable/modular to meet varying service needs.
- All components must be Year 2000 compliant.

**Major Risks/Undecided Issues**
- Automatic launch and recovery system capability is designated as an objective requirement.
