# Ultra Short Summary
A voucher management system to automate STD treatment voucher distribution, claims processing, and provider reimbursement for Marie Stopes International Uganda's OBA program.

- **MVP points**: System generates unique barcoded vouchers with security protocols; captures voucher distribution to clients via distributors; processes treatment claims from service providers with validation; generates payment reports based on approved claims.
- **Key constraints**: Built on Oracle 9i with VB front-end and Crystal Reports 9; high security with granular user access controls; interfaces with barcode and biometric thumbprint readers.
- **Major risks/undecided issues**: Fraud detection in claims relies on thumbprint matching and voucher tracking; Not mentioned.
