# Ultra Short Summary

The Virtual Company Dossier (VCD) provides interoperable solutions for economic operators to electronically submit company qualification documents to public sector awarding entities across EU Member States, enabling cross-border participation in public procurement.

- **MVP points**: The VCD enables economic operators to compile and submit electronic qualification documents; it supports cross-border mutual recognition of certificates and attestations; it provides a structured package containing required evidences; it integrates with the PEPPOL infrastructure for secure document exchange.
- **Key constraints**: The solution must comply with EU Directive 2004/18/EC on public procurement; it must ensure semantic interoperability across different national systems; it must address varying levels of digital maturity among Member States.
- **Major risks**: Not mentioned.
