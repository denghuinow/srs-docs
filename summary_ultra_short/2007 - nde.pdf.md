# Ultra Short Summary

The NDE system processes and distributes NPOESS satellite data to NOAA and civilian users, generating tailored and NOAA-unique products while ensuring archival and customer support.

- **MVP points**
  - Receive and process data from the Interface Data Processing Segment (IDPS).
  - Generate and distribute NOAA-unique and tailored data products.
  - Archive NOAA-unique products to the CLASS long-term archive.
  - Provide customer services including help desk and user notifications.

- **Key constraints**
  - Must comply with Federal Enterprise Architecture (FEA) and ESPC security procedures.
  - System must support daily product volumes of 4 TB input and 8 TB output during NPP mission.
  - Operational availability must not be interrupted for more than 2 hours in any 24-hour period.
